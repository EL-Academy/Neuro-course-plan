Course Overview

Level: 3rd semseterCredits: 3 (2 Theory + 1 Practical)Duration: 16 Weeks

This lecturer plan provides a structured path through central and peripheral nervous system anatomy with clinical correlation, neuroimaging emphasis, and lesion localization, based on Clinical Neuroanatomy (30th ed., 2024).

Course Objectives

  • Describe structures and functional organization of the CNS and PNS.
  • Trace major sensory and motor pathways and link them to clinical signs.
  • Interpret common neuroimaging and localize lesions clinically.
  • Explain embryologic development and common congenital abnormalities of the CNS.
  • Integrate neuroanatomy knowledge into problem- and case-based learning.
